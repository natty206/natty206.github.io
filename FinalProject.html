Natty's Final Project: Alarm Clock!

I am a hard sleeper and I almost always can sleep through any type of alarm. I wanted to use this project as an opportunity to create an alarm clock that could help me wake up.
The alarm clock tells me the current time, day of week, and allows me to set an alarm at my desired time.

Here's am intial sketch of my idea:

To bring this project together the different materials I used are:

  • Arduino Uno
  • Crystal Liquid Display (LCD)
  • 6 Buttons
  • Red LED
  • LED light strip
  • Buzzer
  • Potentiometer
  • 2 Resistors
  • Wires
  • Wood to laser Cut the box

2D Model of the box for the alarm clock was designed in Rhino and Laser cut out of wood.
